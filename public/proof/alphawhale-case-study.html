Case Study

How AlphaWhale turned AI invisibility into a measurable citation lift

Before: 12% citation shareAfter: 34% citation shareWindow: 45 days

AEOSpark identified the exact prompts where competitors were being recommended instead of AlphaWhale. The highest leverage changes were adding clearer comparison pages, direct-answer commercial copy, and trust proof on wallet-protection pages.

Within 45 days, Claude citations rose from 3/20 to 8/20 prompts and ChatGPT citations rose from 2/20 to 6/20 prompts. The most important result was not the score change; it was the shift in buyer-intent prompts where AlphaWhale had previously been absent.
